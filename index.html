Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how NutriTools collects, uses, shares, retains, and protects information when you use the NutriTools Android app.

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to NutriTools. NutriTools helps users search nutrition facts and view food nutrient information. The app is supported by ads and uses Google consent tools where required by law.

Information We Collect

NutriTools does not require you to create an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, SMS, call logs, health records, payment information, or government identification numbers.

The app may process the following information to provide and improve the service:

  • Search queries: food names or terms you type into the nutrition search field.
  • Food selections: food item identifiers requested when you open a nutrition detail view.
  • Technical and network information: information normally sent with internet requests, such as IP address, request time, app version, device type, operating system, and diagnostic information.
  • Advertising and consent information: data processed by Google Mobile Ads and Google User Messaging Platform, such as ad identifiers, consent choices, approximate location derived from IP address, device information, and ad interaction data, depending on your region and settings.

How We Use Information

  • To return nutrition search results and food detail information.
  • To keep the app and backend service reliable and secure.
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix technical issues.
  • To show ads and manage ad consent choices where applicable.
  • To comply with legal, safety, and policy obligations.

Sharing Information

We do not sell personal or sensitive user data. Information may be shared only with the categories of parties described below:

  • Service providers: hosting, infrastructure, analytics, diagnostics, and similar providers that help operate the app and backend service.
  • Google advertising services: Google Mobile Ads and Google User Messaging Platform may process advertising, device, consent, and diagnostic data according to Google policies and your consent choices.
  • Legal and safety reasons: information may be disclosed if required by law, legal process, security investigation, or to protect users, the app, or the public.
  • Business transfers: information may be transferred if the app or related service is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or sale of assets, with legally appropriate notice where required.

Third-Party Services

NutriTools uses third-party services that may collect information under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google advertising technologies
  • Google AdMob privacy and data information

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information processed by NutriTools. Information sent between the app and remote services is transmitted using modern transport security where supported. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Retention and Deletion

NutriTools does not create user accounts. Search queries and food detail requests are used to return results and may appear in temporary operational logs. We retain operational logs only as long as reasonably necessary for security, debugging, service reliability, legal compliance, or abuse prevention.

Because NutriTools does not maintain user accounts, there is no app account to delete. You can clear app data from your Android device settings. You may also contact us to request deletion of information that can reasonably be associated with you.

Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app or uninstall it at any time.
  • You can clear local app data through Android system settings.
  • Where available, you can manage ad privacy choices through the privacy options shown in the app or through your device and Google account settings.
  • You can reset or delete your Android advertising ID in your device settings where supported by your Android version.

Children's Privacy

NutriTools is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended for use by children without appropriate parental or guardian involvement. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through NutriTools, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

International Users

Information may be processed in countries other than the country where you live. Where required, we rely on legally appropriate safeguards for cross-border processing and service provider access.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of NutriTools after an updated policy becomes effective means the updated policy applies to your use of the app.
